Services

What we build, and why it holds.

Four practice areas that cover the full surface of a growing Salesforce org — from the foundation up to the AI running on top of it.

01

Architecture

We restructure the org so it holds up as you grow — the data model, permissions, and integrations an early admin never had the time or mandate to design properly.

  • Org and data-model design
  • Permission and sharing model
  • Technical-debt remediation
  • Integration and managed-package review
  • Sandbox and release process
02

Automation

The repetitive work that eats your team's week — quoting, approvals, document generation, follow-ups — moved out of spreadsheets and into the system, where it runs itself.

  • Flow and approval automation
  • Document and contract generation
  • Notifications and task routing
  • Data hygiene and de-duplication
  • Reporting and dashboards
03

Agentforce & AI

Production AI agents grounded in your own data and playbooks — knowledge assistants and copilots that reach real users, with the guardrails to be trusted there.

  • Agentforce agents grounded in your data
  • Data Cloud setup and indexing
  • Knowledge base preparation
  • Prompt, action, and topic design
  • Production validation and guardrails
04

Go-to-market

The revenue engine — CPQ, pipeline, and intake — rebuilt around how your team actually sells, so quoting and forecasting keep pace instead of slowing everyone down.

  • CPQ and quoting
  • Pipeline and forecasting
  • Lead and intake routing
  • Territory and assignment
  • Integration with billing and finance
How we work

How engagements work

Two ways to work together. Both sized for the mid-market.

No procurement gauntlet and no enterprise invoice — a model that fits a growing company's budget and pace.

Retainer

A monthly partner

A set amount of build capacity each month, applied to a steady backlog. Best when there's always a next thing to ship and you want one partner who already knows the org.

Fixed scope

A defined project

A specific outcome with a defined price and timeline, agreed before we start. Best for a clear build — a CPQ rollout, an intake rebuild, a first Agentforce agent.
