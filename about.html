About

A boutique practice, built on restraint.

San Benito Tech Partners takes its name from a place where the city meets the coast — refined, modern, unhurried. The work tries to feel the same.

The practice

Specialist focus, without the integrator overhead.

San Benito works in that gap. We stay deliberately small and deliberately deep — a few industries, a clear set of services, and modern tooling that lets one focused practice deliver what used to take a team. The result is work that holds up, delivered at a pace and price a mid-market company can live with.

Who you work with

One architect, accountable end to end.

The practice is led by a Salesforce architect who works across the whole system — from data model to automation to Agentforce — and stays with your project from the first call to the hand-off. You always know who is doing the work, because it's the same person you talked to. Delivered in English or Spanish.
