## Brand system — "Horizon" v1.0
- **Colors (6 total):** Ink #0A1A28 · Pacific Navy #1B3A57 · Slate #3D4A5C · Sunset Amber #C97A2C · Sand #EFE4D0 · Paper #F8F1E2. Use **one amber accent per surface**.
- **Type:** Display = Source Serif 4, *italic*, sentence case. Body = Inter. Labels/eyebrows = JetBrains Mono, uppercase, tracked.
- **Mark:** amber sun above a navy horizon line.
- **Voice:** restraint. Say less, mean more. Client-and-firm focused — never ego or founder-as-hero. No internal stack dumps or job-description lists in client-facing copy.

## Design decisions (locked)
- Layout = "Direction C": editorial split hero with a navy "What we do" panel.
- Background rhythm: paper → sand → navy, used deliberately. Two bg colors max per page beyond navy bands.
- Proof is **anonymized**: "a national personal-injury firm" (legal — intake + Agentforce); "a multi-site commercial-services operator" (4 hrs → 4 min on contract generation). Solar is framed as an active focus, not a finished result.
- Pricing: show the model (retainer vs. fixed scope), **no hard numbers**.
- Verticals: Solar · Legal · Commercial services.
